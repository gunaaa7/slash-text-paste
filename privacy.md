PastePal Privacy Policy
PastePal stores the shortcuts and snippet text you create locally in your browser using Chrome extension storage. PastePal does not sell, transmit, or share your data with third parties. PastePal does not collect browsing history, page content, or personally identifiable information.
If you uninstall the extension, locally stored data may be removed by Chrome.
